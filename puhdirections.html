Directions for Hospital Patients

1: Leave elevator from parking garage and turn right 2: Walk all the way to waiting area for HVI and GI. You are on Level 3 3: Go to the elevators on your left and choose Level 5. The elevator panel will tell you which letter elevator to use (the letter is above the elevator door) 4: On Level 5, turn towards the hallway away from the double doors and turn right towards the nursing unit. 5: Walk all the way to nurses' station, then turn LEFT and follow the long hallway. 6: Continue walking straight until you see "5B HVI Clinic". 7: You have arrived at the HVI Clinic.
